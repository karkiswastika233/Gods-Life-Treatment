ENT Treatment

Advanced diagnostics and personalized treatment plans for ENT patients


About ENT

ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) specialists diagnose and treat disorders of the ear, nose, throat, and related structures of the head and neck. Our ENT department provides comprehensive care for conditions ranging from sinus issues to hearing loss.

Our team of experienced otolaryngologists uses the latest diagnostic tools and treatment techniques to provide personalized care for each patient.

Common Symptoms

  • Hearing loss or tinnitus
  • Chronic sinusitis or nasal congestion
  • Frequent sore throats or tonsillitis
  • Vertigo or balance problems
  • Voice changes or hoarseness
  • Snoring or sleep apnea
  • Ear pain or infections
  • Difficulty swallowing

Treatment Options

Click on each treatment option to learn more about our specialized approaches:

Surgery

About Surgical Treatment

Surgery is often the primary treatment for many ENT conditions, especially when tumors or structural abnormalities are present. Our surgeons use minimally invasive techniques whenever possible to reduce recovery time and improve outcomes.

Common ENT surgeries include:

  • Tonsillectomy and adenoidectomy
  • Sinus surgery (endoscopic)
  • Thyroid and parathyroid surgery
  • Head and neck cancer surgery
  • Cochlear implantation
  • Septoplasty and rhinoplasty
Radiation

About Radiation Therapy

Radiation therapy uses high-energy rays to kill cancer cells or shrink tumors. For ENT cancers, it can be used as a primary treatment or in combination with surgery and chemotherapy.

Advanced techniques we use include:

  • Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT)
  • Stereotactic Body Radiation Therapy (SBRT)
  • Image-Guided Radiation Therapy (IGRT)
  • Brachytherapy for precise targeting
  • Proton therapy for complex cases
Chemotherapy

About Chemotherapy

Chemotherapy uses drugs to destroy cancer cells throughout the body. For ENT cancers, it's often used in combination with other treatments to improve outcomes.

Our approach includes:

  • Neoadjuvant chemotherapy before surgery
  • Concurrent chemotherapy with radiation
  • Adjuvant chemotherapy after surgery
  • Palliative chemotherapy for advanced cases
  • Targeted drug combinations
Targeted Drug Therapy

About Targeted Drug Therapy

Targeted therapy uses drugs that specifically target molecular changes in cancer cells that help them grow, divide, and spread. This approach often has fewer side effects than traditional chemotherapy.

Key benefits include:

  • Precision targeting of cancer cells
  • Reduced damage to healthy cells
  • Personalized based on genetic markers
  • Often used when other treatments fail
  • Can be combined with other therapies
Immunotherapy

About Immunotherapy

Immunotherapy helps your immune system recognize and attack cancer cells. This innovative approach has shown remarkable success in treating various ENT cancers, especially those that have been resistant to other treatments.

Our immunotherapy options include:

  • Checkpoint inhibitors (PD-1/PD-L1 inhibitors)
  • CAR T-cell therapy for specific cancers
  • Cancer vaccines in clinical trials
  • Monoclonal antibodies
  • Combination approaches with other treatments

Dental Treatment

Comprehensive care for all dental and oral health needs

About Dental Care

Our dental department provides comprehensive oral health services, from routine cleanings to complex surgical procedures. We focus on preventive care to maintain your oral health and address issues before they become serious.

Our team of dental specialists includes general dentists, orthodontists, periodontists, and oral surgeons working together to provide complete dental care.

Common Dental Issues

  • Tooth decay and cavities
  • Gum disease (gingivitis and periodontitis)
  • Tooth sensitivity
  • Bad breath (halitosis)
  • Tooth loss
  • Oral cancer
  • Temporomandibular joint disorders (TMJ)
  • Misaligned teeth or bite issues

Treatment Options

Click on each treatment option to learn more about our specialized approaches:

Fillings and Restorations

About Fillings and Restorations

Dental fillings are used to repair cavities and restore the function and integrity of tooth structure. We use tooth-colored composite materials that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.

Our restoration services include:

  • Composite (tooth-colored) fillings
  • Inlays and onlays for larger cavities
  • Dental crowns for severely damaged teeth
  • Bonding for chipped or discolored teeth
  • Minimally invasive techniques
Root Canal Therapy

About Root Canal Therapy

Root canal treatment is used to save a tooth that is severely decayed or infected. The procedure involves removing the damaged area of the tooth (the pulp), cleaning and disinfecting it, then filling and sealing it.

Benefits of root canal therapy:

  • Preserves your natural tooth
  • Efficient and relatively comfortable
  • Prevents spread of infection
  • Restores normal biting force and sensation
  • Maintains natural appearance
